# ⚔️ Imperium-Conqueror: The Age of Empires

**Imperium-Conqueror** is a turn-based grand strategy game developed in Java. Players assume the role of a Commander, managing one of three historical capitals—**Cairo, Rome, or Sparta**—with the ultimate goal of global hegemony. 

The project showcases advanced **Swing UI customization**, a **procedural Audio Engine**, and a robust **MVC (Model-View-Controller)** architecture.

---

## 📜 Field Manual (How to Play)

### 1. Goal
Conquer all enemy cities on the map within **30 turns**. If you fail to capture all territories or lose your capital, the empire falls.

### 2. Economic Management
*   **Gold 💰:** Generated by your cities. Used to build structures and recruit specialized units.
*   **Food 🍞:** Essential for maintaining a standing army. Ensure your supply lines are strong before expanding.

### 3. Military & Buildings
*   **Barracks:** Unlock Infantry units.
*   **Stables:** Unlock Cavalry for high mobility.
*   **Archery Range:** Unlock Archers for tactical advantages.
*   **Upgrades:** Level up your cities to increase resource yield and recruitment capacity.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Features

### 🎨 Custom Medieval UI
Unlike standard Java Swing applications, this project uses `Graphics2D` to render:
*   Rounded, semi-transparent panels.
*   Golden-gradient buttons and medieval-style progress bars.
*   Custom-loaded fonts and high-quality game icons.

### 🎵 Dynamic Audio Engine
The game features a context-aware sound system:
*   **Procedural Music:** The soundtrack shifts intensity based on your treasury level and whether your cities are under siege.
*   **SFX:** High-fidelity click, victory, and defeat sounds.

### 🗺️ Strategic Map System
A visual overview dialog that renders your empire's reach, allowing you to see which cities are owned, neutral, or enemy-controlled at a glance.
